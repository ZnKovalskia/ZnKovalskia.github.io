Black Ivory

Black Ivory coffee is a brand of coffee produced by Black Ivory Coffee Company Ltd in northern Thailand, which is processed from Arabica coffee beans that have been eaten by elephants and collected from their droppings.The taste of this coffee is influenced by digestive enzymes in elephants, which break down the proteins in the coffee.Protein is one of the main factors in coffee bitterness. With less protein, the bitterness is also reduced.
